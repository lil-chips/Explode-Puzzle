Activity 3: Project charter for LOS case study
The Project Charter for LOS was created under an assumption
that the project will be conducted using Agile approach.
If we would plan for a traditional project, the WBS (work
breakdown structure) must be created in a different way.
Discuss with the tutor the differences and similarities on the
basis of LOS case study.
Duration: approx. 30 minutes

Activity 4: WBS
Scenario: Assume that the LOS project should be
conducted using a traditional methodology, within 6
months (1 May – 31 October).
Task 1: Discuss with the tutor what information is missing in
the scenario to start creating WBS. Extend the scenario
based on this discussion.
Task 2: Create a WBS with at least two levels for the case
✓ Think of general steps that may be needed!
✓ Make sure that you included predecessor, task durations,
resources and milestones.
✓ The tasks must be at least two levelled.
Templates are available in Canvas:
Modules →  Templates, Tools, Team Allocation →
Useful templates
Duration: approx. 30 minutes
