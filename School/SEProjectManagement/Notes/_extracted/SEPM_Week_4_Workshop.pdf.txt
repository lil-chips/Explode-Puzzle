===== Page 5 =====
Detailed checklist to analyse the product backlog
1. Are the PBI IDs unique?
2. Do the PBI IDs have a reasonable format?
3. Do PBI have a correct user story format (As a… I want to …
so that …)?
4. Are the items in the product backlog presented in a correct
order?
5. Are the priorities correct wrt. the scenario?
6. Are the numbers of story points allocated to each PBI
reasonable?
7. Is the syntax of efforts correct (Fibonacci numbers are
used)?
8. Are/Is the DoD(s) reasonable?
9. Are there any contradictions among the PBIs or within the
PBIs?
10. Is the product backlog complete?
11. Are all the PBIs in scope?
These two points will be
discussed on Week 5
