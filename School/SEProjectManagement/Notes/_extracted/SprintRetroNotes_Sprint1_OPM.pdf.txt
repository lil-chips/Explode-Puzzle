SPRINT RETRO NOTES DATE OF THE MEETING:   10 MARCH 2022
Project: OPM (Online Patient Management)  Team: X1   Attended Absent Scrum Master Severus Snape  Development Team Harry Potter, Ron Weasley, Hermione Granger, Neville Longbottom, Luna Lovegood, Draco Malfoy
Sprint #: 1  1. What went well? We completed all PBIs planned for this sprint and were able to implement two extra PBI from the top of the product backlog. We were able to use Selenium for automated testing – this was our first time using it for a real project!  2. What went not so well? Estimation of the team’s capacity: we underestimated the team’s capacity. Demo: The demo at the Sprint review meeting was not so good, the customers understood only at the very end of the meeting what exactly is now fully completed. Also, the battery of the laptop on which we were planning to present became empty at the middle of demo.  3. Lessons learned Estimates: We can do more than 15 story points per sprint. Demo: We need to have a team-internal dry-run of the demo before the Sprint review meeting. We need to check whether the demo-laptop is charged and that we have a back-up laptop also prepared for demo. We haven’t done automated testing before, and we were surprised how much time we saved on regression testing. Things to Keep: Automated testing with Selenium Things to Change: We will plan for 25 story points as team’s velocity, plan team-internal dry-run of the demo, backups for demo.
