Scrum using Trello =
Scrum + Kanban
Columns
▪ Product backlog  – or  – Project backlog
▪ Sprint Backlog
▪ In progress
▪ Ready to present
▪ Done – or  – Sprint 1 Done, Sprint 2 Done, ….
Cards
▪ Should be allocated, when ”In progress”
▪ Labels might be useful

▪ Design, Implementation, T esting (or several items for
several types of testing), Deployment, …
▪ Can be used to reflect DoD
