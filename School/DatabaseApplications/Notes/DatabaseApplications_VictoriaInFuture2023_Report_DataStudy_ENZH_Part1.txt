====================================================================
1) What is “Victoria in Future 2023” (VIF2023)?
====================================================================

EN: VIF2023 is the official Victorian Government projection of population and households.
ZH: VIF2023 是维多利亚州政府官方的人口与住户（household）预测。

EN: These projections are *estimates*, not exact forecasts and not targets. Uncertainty increases over longer horizons and smaller geographic areas.
ZH: 这些预测是「估计」而非精确预测，也不是政策目标。时间越长、地理范围越小，不确定性越大。

EN: Projections are used by government/business/community to plan for growth and change.
ZH: 预测用于政府/商业/社区规划资源与服务，应对人口规模和结构变化。

Exam trap
EN: If asked “is this a forecast?”, answer: it’s a projection based on assumptions; not a guarantee.
ZH: 若问「是不是预测（forecast）？」应答：是基于假设的 projection，不是保证。

====================================================================
2) Data sources & what “ERP” means
====================================================================

EN: The projections are based on Estimated Resident Population (ERP) — the ABS official population estimate.
ZH: 预测基于 ERP（Estimated Resident Population，估计常住人口）——ABS 的官方人口估计。

EN: Latest baseline used: numbers for 30 June 2022 (per report text).
ZH: 报告使用的最新基准：截至 2022 年 6 月 30 日的数据。

EN: Household and dwelling data are based on the 2021 Census results.
ZH: 住户与住房（dwelling）数据基于 2021 人口普查。

DB-minded note
EN: ERP is a *definition*, not just a column. In your schema, include metadata: source=ABS ERP, reference date, revision/version.
ZH: ERP 是一种「定义/口径」，不仅仅是一列数字。建库时应包含元数据：来源=ABS ERP、参考日期、版本/修订信息。

====================================================================
3) Publication granularity (important for tables you may download)
====================================================================

EN: State-level projections: published for every year, with detailed demographic data from 2011 to 2051.
ZH: 州级预测：每年都有，并含 2011–2051 的详细人口数据。

EN: Sub-state projections:
- Major regions: every 5th year from 2021 Census, extend to 2051.
- Local Government Areas (LGAs): extend to 2036 (per report).
ZH: 次州级预测：
- 主要区域：从 2021 Census 起每 5 年一次，延伸到 2051。
- LGA：延伸到 2036。

Exam trap
EN: Don’t assume all geographies have annual data to 2051. LGA series may stop at 2036.
ZH: 不要假设所有地理层级都能按年到 2051。LGA 可能只到 2036。

4) Highlights: headline numbers (Victoria 2023→2051)
====================================================================

EN: Victoria total population: 6.8m (2023) → 10.3m (2051).
ZH: 维州总人口：2023 年 680 万 → 2051 年 1030 万。

EN: Metropolitan Melbourne: 5.1m → 8.0m.
ZH: 墨尔本大都会：510 万 → 800 万。

EN: Regional Victoria: 1.7m → 2.3m.
ZH: 维州地区（非大都会）：170 万 → 230 万。

EN: Housing: +1.5m additional households; +1.6m additional dwellings (from infographic).
ZH: 住房：新增住户约 150 万；新增住房约 160 万。

DB-minded note
EN: These are “top-level aggregates”. They should be derivable from underlying tables (by summing). If not, your imported data may be incomplete or differently scoped.
ZH: 这些是顶层汇总值，应可从底层表汇总得到。若对不上，可能是导入数据不全或口径不同。

5) Components of population change (cohort component model)
====================================================================

5.1 The model
EN: Population projections rely on the cohort component model. Population change is driven by:
- Natural increase = births − deaths
- Net overseas migration (NOM)
- Net interstate migration (NIM)
ZH: 人口预测使用 cohort component model（队列组成模型）。人口变化由三部分驱动：
- 自然增长=出生−死亡
- 海外净迁移 NOM
- 州际净迁移 NIM

5.2 Victoria 2023→2051 components (from report figure)
EN: The report’s figure shows (approx):
- Births: 2.73m
- Deaths: 1.67m
→ Natural increase: +1.06m
- Overseas migration: +2.34m
- Interstate migration: +0.13m
→ Net migration: +2.47m
ZH: 图示给出（约）：
- 出生 273 万
- 死亡 167 万
→ 自然增长 +106 万
- 海外迁移 +234 万
- 州际迁移 +13 万
→ 净迁移 +247 万

Interpretation
EN: The majority of growth is projected to come from migration (especially NOM), not just natural increase.
ZH: 人口增长主要来自迁移（尤其海外净迁移），而不仅是自然增长。

Exam trap
EN: People often say “births cause growth” — but the report explicitly quantifies that migration dominates.
ZH: 很多人会直觉说「主要靠出生」。但报告量化显示迁移贡献更大。

6) Migration assumptions and COVID disruption
====================================================================

EN: The report notes migration patterns were disrupted during COVID. Victoria lost population through overseas and interstate migration during the pandemic.
ZH: 报告提到 COVID 期间迁移模式被打乱：海外与州际迁移都曾导致人口净流失。

EN: It assumes a rapid return of overseas migration: two years of elevated arrivals and strong NOM, followed by a smaller-than-average NOM in 2024/25.
ZH: 假设海外迁移快速回归：两年高峰，然后 2024/25 NOM 低于平均。

EN: Over the longer term, NOM is expected to resemble pre-COVID patterns, adding ~82,000–84,000 people per year.
ZH: 长期 NOM 回到疫情前水平，每年增加约 8.2–8.4 万。

EN: NIM is expected to return positive and settle around 5,100 per annum from 2028 onwards.
ZH: NIM 预计恢复为正，并从 2028 起稳定在每年约 5100。

DB-minded note
EN: These are assumptions, not measurements. Store them separately from observed historical data, and tag the projection scenario/version.
ZH: 这些是「假设」而非「观测」。数据库中应与历史观测数据分表或分标记，并记录情景/版本。

7) Fertility, mortality, life expectancy assumptions
====================================================================

EN: Fertility assumption: women will have on average 1.5 children over their lifetime.
ZH: 生育假设：女性平均终身生育 1.5 个孩子。

EN: Births per year projected: 78,000 (2023) → 112,000 (2051).
ZH: 年出生数：2023 年 7.8 万 → 2051 年 11.2 万。

EN: Deaths per year projected: 46,000 (2023) → 74,000 (2051) despite increased life expectancy, because population is larger and older.
ZH: 年死亡数：2023 年 4.6 万 → 2051 年 7.4 万。尽管预期寿命提高，但人口规模更大且更老龄化。

EN: Life expectancy at birth projected:
- Males: 82 → 86
- Females: 86 → 89
ZH: 出生时预期寿命：
- 男：82→86
- 女：86→89

Common mistake
EN: “If life expectancy increases, deaths should decrease.” Not necessarily: total deaths can rise if population and elderly share grow.
ZH: 「寿命变长，死亡数应该下降」不一定成立：人口变多、老年占比提高，总死亡数仍会增加。

8) Distribution & LGA highlights (what to notice)
====================================================================

EN: Fastest projected growth LGA (2021–2036): Mitchell Shire at 6.0% p.a., adding 70,100 people; much growth within Metro Melbourne Urban Growth Boundary.
ZH: 增长最快 LGA（2021–2036）：Mitchell Shire 年均 6.0%，新增 70100 人；增长主要在大墨尔本增长边界内。

EN: Top five projected population growth (2021–2036):
Metro: Wyndham (175,900), Melton (168,200), Casey (158,000), Hume (109,200), Whittlesea (103,600)
Regional: Greater Geelong (96,900), Ballarat (31,200), Greater Bendigo (28,300), Baw Baw (21,600), Moorabool (18,900)
ZH: 2021–2036 增长前五：
大都会：Wyndham、Melton、Casey、Hume、Whittlesea（数值见上）
区域：Greater Geelong、Ballarat、Greater Bendigo、Baw Baw、Moorabool。

DB-minded note
EN: LGAs are a geography dimension. Use stable identifiers (e.g., ABS LGA codes) rather than names only (names can change).
ZH: LGA 属于地理维度。最好用稳定 ID（如 ABS LGA code），不要只用名称（名称可能变化）。
