In this case, you have to evaluate the filtering condition on each group, not on each row in your joined input. To evaluate a condition on the group (i.e. condition on GROUPed result), we use HAVING clause.   Write a SQL statement to assist Penny to obtain the required data from the database.
SELECT d.dirname, SUM(m.awrd), SUM(m.noms)      FROM movie m JOIN director d ON m.dirnumb = d.dirnumb      GROUP BY d.dirname      WHERE sum(m.noms) >= 1;
